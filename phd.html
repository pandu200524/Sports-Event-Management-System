PhD Scholars Sports Meet

Sport's league where research meets resilience


Event Overview

The PhD Scholars Sports Meet conducted various sports from intense chess and carrom games to thrilling volleyball, badminton, table tennis matches, the day was filled with energy, teamwork, and competitive spirit. The PhD scholars show that excellence isn't just in academics but in every move, match, and game. This is hosted by SRM University AP to showcase skills and experience the thrill of competition.

Event Highlights

  • Various sports competitions among PhD scholars of all departments
  • Interactive workshops
  • Sports merchandise and events
  • Prize distribution ceremony

Sports Conducted

  • Carroms
  • Chess
  • Table tennis
  • Badminton
  • Volleyball
