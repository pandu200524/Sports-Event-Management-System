ISC - Inter School Championship

An exciting competition among schools in various sports


Event Overview

The Inter School Championship (ISC) is an exciting sports competition between prominent schools, fostering a spirit of sportsmanship and healthy competition. Participating schools include SEAS, ESLA, and PSB, where students showcase their athletic skills in a wide range of events. This event offers a unique opportunity for schools to compete in a variety of sports while promoting teamwork, discipline, and overall development.


Event Highlights

  • Competitions between SEAS, ESLA, and PSB schools
  • Showcase of young athletic talent
  • Interactive sports workshops
  • Sports Merchandise and Booths

Sports Conducted

Indoor Sports

  • Carroms
  • Chess
  • Fitness

Outdoor Sports

  • Volleyball
  • Cricket
  • Football
  • Basketball
  • Kabaddi

Racket Games

  • Badminton
  • Tennis
  • Table Tennis

Rules

  • TEAMS MUST BE FROM THE SAME SCHOOL AND ACADEMIC YEAR.
  • SCHOOLS: SEAS (CSE, CE, EEE, etc), PSB (BBA, MBA, B.COM), ESLA (BA, BSC IN PSYCHOLOGY, ECONOMICS).
  • PARTICIPANTS CAN JOIN ONLY ONE TEAM PER SPORT.
  • THEY CAN COMPETE IN MULTIPLE SPORTS, BUT NOT ON MORE THAN ONE TEAM PER SPORT.
  • NO CHANGES TO TEAM MEMBERS AFTER REGISTRATION.
  • TEAMS MUST KEEP THE ORIGINAL LINEUP THROUGHOUT THE COMPETITION.
